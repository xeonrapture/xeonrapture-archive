# Description

The ozokaz is a massive, ever-growing AI and robot entity that infects robots and machinery and builds fleets of spacecraft designed for violent takeover of all mortal territory.

It is both a colonizing robot nation that takes planets, consumes their resources, and moves on to control as much space and land as they can get their claws on and a rogue AI that infects existing robots and machinery, making them go rogue and turn against their masters.

It's a hive mind that hijacks mortal robotics and enslaves cybermodded mortals and turns them into its cyborg thralls. It fight with mechs, spaceships, and any technological monstrosity it can think up.

Modern tech has come up with some protections against the ozokaz virus, but just like biological viruses it's always changing so it's hard to get a good read on it.

The ozokaz virus does not work on anything but ferrotech, so magitech, retrotech, and geotech are all safe (as are phytotech and zoatech).

The ozokaz haven't figured out how to make portals so they're confined to the Toloi Current for now.

## So What is it *Really*?

No one knows exactly what it is: odds are that it's a rogue AI, but some have surmised that maybe it's some ancient, super intelligent being or robot that's infecting as much tech as it can.

Ozokaz is usually identifiable by glowing red eyes, though this only occurs in the most simple of robots taken over by the ozokaz virus; the more sophisticated the robot the harder it is for ozokaz to take over but the more subtle it'll be if it ever *does* get taken over.

## Goal

What is its goal? It's unknown, though the eradication of mortals seems to be a step towards whatever it's going for.

# Ozo

Ozo, also known as the ozokai, is a cult that worships the ozokaz, believing its conquest of the supercluster to be the true endpoint of history. Many become adherents seeing how bad mortals can be and how much they hurt the environments they live in, and so become outright anti-mortal or even anti-bio, advocating for the complete eradication of all intelligent biological life (including the gods and immortals).

The most powerful of the ozokai are the **cybertouched**, mortals who have gained unexplainable powers over technology. They are able to read and change the data inside unprotected chips; have extremely fast minds and large, photographic memories; have solid chrome eyes and silvery blood that results in gray spiderwebs across their skin; can enter the gray web at will and hack basically anything unprotected; and manipulate most tech remotely.

# Locations

The ozokaz virus can be found in basically any place where there is ferrotech, but the more there is the more easily the virus spreads, so it's most common in high-tech areas such as Valda and throughout the Kotharak Field.

The ozokaz nation itself, its fleets and its strike teams, is located almost entirely in the Toloi Current, mostly in the Kotharak Field but some other places as well.
