# Description

Strobe is the fun gang, dealing more with psychedelics and other crazy experiences than [[xr/Worldbuilding/Dangers/Lyon|Lyon]] does. They run a massive string of rave clubs all throughout the supercluster and often don't beef with other gangs and corps (at least not outwardly). In fact, most people around the universe know them more as an entertainment giant than as a gang.

Likes to have a large amount of blackmail on hand for all the politicians and executives they may want to bribe, and has a large network of [[Webrunning 2.0|webrunners]] going around, stealing important data and sabotaging their enemies.

Huge proponent of [[Valda#Hedonistic Spiritualism|hedonic spiritualism]].

## Goal

Their goal is to spread their drugs, gain influence everywhere, and low-key to convert others to their way of thinking.

# Locations

Strobe has a presence in all high-tech places and many low-tech places as well, but not really in any locations with a ban on tech.
