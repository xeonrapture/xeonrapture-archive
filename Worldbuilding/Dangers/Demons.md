# Description

Demons are xenic spirits of pure darkness and evil, a natural manifestation of the destructive nature of the universe and the kernel of evil in the heart of every mortal.

They're always working against the light gods and every good mortal and immortal, trying to gain power, to convert people to evil, to weaken the resolve of the good (that being said, most mortal evil thoughts probably do *not* come directly from demons).

Demons are made out of menon, or tainted xeon, and because of this they have a lot of the same powers that xenic spirits do but gross and bent towards destruction and corruption.

These beings are very hard to deal with, as they're literally impossible to kill. The best one can do is to imprison them or banish them to another place or to the [[xr/Worldbuilding/Lore/The Xenic Plane|xenic plane]].

Zythix, the Primordial God of Darkness and Evil, the Elder Demon, the Genesis of Evil, is the purest manifestation of the menon and is the most powerful demon by far (and one of the most dangerous beings in existence, dwarfing all but other primordial gods in power).

## Powers

Demons can create darkness, possess mortal beings (including beasts and intelligent mortals) and corpses, harass and torture the living, and necrose healthy flesh.

If a being is possessed by a powerful demon then they can grow unnaturally, becoming much more powerful and harder to kill. In some places possessed beasts are a huge problem, ravaging the wilderness and making it impossible to live in peace.

## Goal

To destroy everything good, to corrupt the beautiful and the gentle.

# Locations

Demons are everywhere, but they're more subtle in places that don't really believe in demons, because if people believe in them and are superstitious in general they can be more flashy and wield their influence more brazenly.

They tend to be an especially big problem in places like Ydilik and Voroki where people believe in and work with spirits all the time.
