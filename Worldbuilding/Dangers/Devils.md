# Description

Devils are spirits that arise from the xeon because of the strong emotion or an intense event: the xeon is responding to the mortals and forming itself into a manifestation of their feelings.

These spirits can be made from positive or negative emotions, can be formed from a powerful group event or something that just an individual experiences, and they can attach themselves to an individual person or place depending on the emotion they're made of and the event that made them.

Devils can stay at the intelligent level of a beast or can grow in intelligence till they're as intelligent or more intelligent than mortals; the more intelligent ones usually end up influencing or even possessing an individual or moving upon a group of people.

The more mortals sacrifice to them, how much meaning they assign to them, or how much they believe in them the stronger they get. The stronger they get the more physical they can become and the more they can affect the world around them. 

The most powerful devils are ones that have gained power over time by absorbing their emotion: for example, a guardian devil arises from the desire to protect an important monument in a small village, but as the village gets bigger and the monument gets more important the guardian gets stronger. The nastiest or greatest devils are the ones that arise around an event that gains prominence or a myth that spreads, and the belief and emotion that this story or myth evokes goes back to strengthen the devil.

## Appearance

Devils can look humanoid or the farthest thing away from humanoid, but their form *has* to be informed by the event or emotions that formed them. Some of them can also disguise themselves as an animal or object.

## Types

 The more popular emotions for devils to feed on are fear (the most common), anger (the most dangerous), grief, lust, protectiveness (guardians and guardian angels, usually good but can become evil), and joy (sometimes form around a strong event and then bless someone and disappear or stick around to continue to bless the place or people that formed them).

Devils (and other spirits) that latch onto and follow a specific person are called wraiths.

## Grades

Devils are ranked by devil hunter societies such as the Raven and the Hare by a zero to six grading system, where grade zero devils are the strongest and most powerful and grade six devils are the weakest.

Scale describes the area that a devil has power over or threatens, and gives you a picture of the scale of mobilization that would be needed for normal people to take care of it.

| Grade | Power Level             | Avg. Difficulty | Avg. Risk | Scale                                                             |
| ----- | ----------------------- | --------------- | --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0     | Middle God, Titan (S+)  | Godlike (80)    | Suicidal  | Has power over light years of territory                 |
| I     | Lesser God, Dragon (S)  | Dragon (70)     | Extreme   | Has power over a continent or a whole planet            |
| II    | Immortal, Wyvern (A)    | Immortal (60)   | Extreme   | Has power over a whole forest or city (or similar area) |
| III   | Elite enemies (B)       | Elite (50)      | High      | Has power over a whole town or small city               |
| IV    | Very strong enemies (C) | Extreme (40)    | High      | Has power over a town or village                        |
| V     | Strong enemies (D)      | Tricky (30)     | Med.      | Has power over part of a town or village                |
| VI    | Normal enemies (E)      | Hard (20)       | Med.      | Has power over a person, family, or community           |

## Sorcerers

Often the more intelligent devils will choose to enter a pact with a mortal, offering power in return for some sacrifice or recompense. These sacrifices can be anything from just letting the devil follow you or even giving up a body part or years of your life, but these deals grant the mortal on the other side great power.

## Goal

The goal of a devil really depends on what event or emotions they was formed from: they can be pure evil or pure good but more often than not they're morally gray, with goals informed by their creation.

Often devils formed from negative emotions are either trying to exercise their emotions on mortals or resolve their emotions (such as an anger demon hurting mortals, a sadness demon trying to find companionship, a fear demon trying to keep itself safe or inflicting its fear on others).

Devils formed from positive emotions are trying to spread their emotion and exercise it on the world (such as a protectiveness devil keeping those it has attached to safe or a joy or love devil healing and helping people).

# Locations

Devils are found everywhere but show up more often in places where people believe in them.
