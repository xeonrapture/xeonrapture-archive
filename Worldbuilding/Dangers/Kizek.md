# Description

The kizek are a type of being, mortals made immortal but imperfectly, born again but without warmth and with a hunger unlike any mortal hunger.

Their ability to subsist on any food and water is gone and in its place is a need to drink the blood and eat the flesh of their own species. The more they partake the more pale they become, the sharper their incisors get, the less they feel like the person they once was.

All kizek become nocturnal because if they're in the sun too long their skin starts to burn and shrivel, and then steams black smoke and starts falling off. Kizek can eat normal food but they don't have to and they don't feel the pleasure of eating like mortals do (other normal pleasures are reduced as well).

They're unaffected by extreme hot and cold, poison, and acid, and they don't get exhausted like mortals do.

## Blood-Drinker Kizek

There is a large group of kizek that have decided, whether on principle, because of squeamishness, or because they simply can't imagine becoming a cannibal, that they will only drink blood. A small amount try to subsist on the blood of beasts, but this is a poor substitute and these kizek always end up living a half life; the larger group of these kizek drink the blood of their species.

These are usually the less destructive of the two groups of kizek but they still live as parasites, weakening their own people, never able to truly sate their hunger.

## Flesh-Eater Kizek

The smaller, much more dangerous group of kizek eat the flesh of their own species and gradually lose their minds but become much, *much* stronger than the blood-drinkers because of their flesh-eating habits. They turn into monsters.

Over time their pupils get smaller and stay black or turn red, and their iris turns black or red and grows till there's no white left.

There are no even semi-good flesh-eater kizek, their actions preclude them from any gentleness or goodness.

## Powers

All kizek have natural regeneration, though it isn't super fast. They regenerate faster when they sleep.

Depending on their chroma, kizek get different powers when they are made a kizek.

- Reds become very strong and fast, they can make themselves lighter and drop their friction, they can fly, and they're naturally hot to the touch.
- Blues become extremely tough and have even more endurance than normal kizek. They have inertial dampening so they're basically impossible to move, can up their own friction and weight, can use force fields, and are cold to the touch.
- Greens can sense emotions, have extremely heightened senses, and can turn into animals (usually with dark coloring and a bit stronger than the basic animal). They have faster healing, and can heal very fast without sleeping. They have an aphrodisiac bite and can affect emotions in their presence.
- Grays think very, very quickly, can see into the xeon, can do [[Mentalist#Powers|xeon binding]], and make large blades and weapons out of hard gray-black xeon. They can disappear into darkness and appear in the shadows somewhere else.
- Clear chromas become lesser kizek; they are a bit stronger, faster, and tougher than mortals but with all the debuffs and annoyances of being a kizek.

## How Kizek are Made

To make a kizek, one has to rip out the heart of one of their own species, rip open their own body, and replace the victim's heart with a sliver of the black, shiny gem that's grown where their own heart used to be. They also generally eat their victim's heart for an unknown reason (this isn't necessary, and it's the only time the blood-only kizek let themselves eat flesh).

The victim then grows a black gem heart out of the sliver that was placed in their chest, a process that takes a couple days.

The myth is that the dark god Xotl created the first kizek from an aith searching for immortality.

## How Kizek are Killed

To kill a kizek (or for a kizek to kill themselves), you must take out their heart and break it.

For as simple as that sounds it's not so easy: this deed requires a massive amount of force as well as a weapon that uses black-gray xeon (onyx or obsidian) or a weapon enchanted for this specific goal, plus you have to extract it from the kizek to begin with (their skin is only breakable with sharp black-gray xeon). 

It usually takes many powerful hits to break a kizek heart, but once it breaks it shatters into a million pieces which then turn into dust.

Also once you take the heart out the kizek will know where you are the moment you touch the heart with black xeon. They get a bit weaker without their heart, but also more desperate, and if you've gotten to this point you probably haven't seen all a kizek can do.

A kizek cannot regrow their heart, so there are tales of kizek hearts wandering far, far away from their owners, only for an unsuspecting mortal to attempt to break it and summon the kizek who then wreaks havoc.

Good luck.

## Goal

To feed. To find whatever pleasure they can, whether that be physical or in their possessions. To punish the world for hurting them and mortals for having the life they've left behind.

The better ones might have some altruistic or selfish but harmless goal, but their hunger will always distract them.

# Locations

Kizek are extremely sneaky and exist pretty much anywhere (though because they need a population of their own species to feed on they tend to gravitate towards large towns and cities).
