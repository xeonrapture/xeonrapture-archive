# Description

Riot is an anarchist gang that started as an organization with clear political goals and quickly inspired a massive amount of splinter groups that adopted all the aesthetics and none of the politics of the original.

These splinter groups are anarchists in the colloquial sense of the word: extremely violent, anti-government and chaotic just for the catharsis of it all. Riot is uncontrollable and impossible to completely destroy.

## Goal

The core, the original group of Riot's goal is accelerationist: to sow chaos and kill upper-ranked officials of all oppressive governments and organizations in order to foment working class violence, property destruction, and revolution.

The splinter groups tend to just love chaos and violence for its own sake.

# Locations

There are fragments of Riot everywhere, but the core group is very small and mostly only active in certain pockets of the Kotharak Field.
