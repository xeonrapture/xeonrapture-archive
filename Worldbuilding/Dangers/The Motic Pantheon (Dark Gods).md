# Description

The Motic Pantheon is the dark mirror of the Archonic Pantheon: they want to destroy, torture, and enslave mortals, not save them, and are diametrically opposed to everything the Archons and their pantheon stands for.

This dark pantheon is led by Mot, The Corrupt One, a greater god fallen away into madness and evil. He drew all his children along with him, immortal and mortal, and leads a slew of middle gods, dragons, and evil archonic mortals that he's seduced away with promises of power and wealth.

## Mot

The God of Dark Wisdom, The Corrupt One, Gethenogoth

Mot is the leader of the Motic Pantheon, the genesis of all the hate and destruction that he sows across the supercluster.

Has only really been seen as a hulking figure in the shadows; prefers to have his servants show themselves instead of him.

He has almost unlimited powers of destruction and corruption, and can enslave mortals to his will (though he has to persuade them to give him power in if the mortal is under the protection of an Archon, which most archonic mortals are). He can also communicate through dreams and create portals to other locations in the physical plane and into the xenic plane at will.

The rumor is that Mot is related in some way to the Archons or the Archons' progenitor, but no one really knows how.

## Mot's Children, the Motic Middle Gods

The Motic Pantheon is full of some of the most evil gods that exist, all bent towards the destruction of the Archons and their mortals.

### Ngyer-ein 

Pronounced "nn-gai-er-ein"

The Right Hand of Mot

Assists Mot in all things. Devilish and crafty, mischievous, totally without mercy. In charge of the spies that keep an eye on everyone.

Usually disguised as a thin-faced, lanky mortal of any species. Thinks very quickly and is extremely intelligent, has info transfer and telepathy, has very good senses, and can shield himself.

### Sveh 

Pronounced "ss-veh"

Mot's Eyes

A venothian dragon that rules over all the other venothian dragons under Mot (more details on venothian dragons coming soon).

### Qzoth 

Pronounced "kuh-zoth"

God of War

Creates and incites war. Bloody and brutal.

Over the years has destroyed countless civilizations by inciting them against each other or dividing them from within. Stories from the First age describe him taking on whole armies by himself.

Appears as a massive, ten-foot-tall, red-skinned humanoid with horns, claws, and needle teeth. He wears nothing but tanned skins over his muscles and thick skin and wields a battle mace as tall as him.

Has uranium destruction powers, heavy shielding, and has insane strength speed, stamina and toughness, and has very high senses. Can buff those around him with strength, speed, stamina, and toughness. Can portal at will.

### Txazuk 

Pronounced "took-zah-zook"

God of Tricks

Likes playing with mortals, killing, torturing, but always within a game or a riddle.

Loves cheating at his own games, tricking people into bad deals, trapping mortals in endless mazes and forcing them to do heinous things.

Appears as a humanoid with a jackal head.

Has incredible shapeshifting and cloaking powers.

### Uulith-btha 

Pronounced "oo-lith-buh-thaw"

Goddex of Greed

Takes anything and everything she wants. Collects visages, so no one has seen their true form.

Lives as a human woman or man with a massive amount of possessions and money.

Also likes collecting people and forcing them to give everything up for him.

Has intense emotional and mental manipulation powers.

### Xotl 

Pronounced "kzaw-tul"

God of Madness, God of Forbidden Knowledge

Obsessed with dark knowledge, lies, deceit, and confusion. Loves to give mortals more than they can handle and make them lose their minds.

Experiments with menon and its effects on the mortal and immortal mind; legends say that Xotl created the first [[Kizek|kizek]].

Stories say Xotl appears as a many-limbed humanoid dressed in pure white rags.

Has extensive menon powers; can do all the things magicians and possessors can do plus a lot more. Can enslave minds, mutate, and poison; create destruction and madness; communicate through dreams, sap life force, and make portals to the physical and xenic planes.

Rumors are that Xotl is imprisoned in a hidden place where he schemes and does all he can to inflict his will from afar.

### Zvithwa

Pronounced "zu-vith-wah"

Goddess of Chaos

She sows chaos, division, and confusion everywhere she can; she's devious and shifty and you can never quite know what she's thinking.

Has a few trusted servants who do her bidding to destabilize governments and spread amorality.

Appears as an extremely attractive, purple-skinned, black-haired woman.

Can cloak, use telepathy, create very potent poisons and venoms, shapeshift, and portal into the physical and xenic planes.

She runs the Chaos Pact.

## Goal

Enslave the Archons and their mortals, destroy everything they stand for, and gain total control over the whole supercluster.

# Locations

The Motic Gods have their tendrils all across the Isvah Supercluster.
