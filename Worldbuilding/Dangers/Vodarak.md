# Description

The vodarak are a bug-like alien people with wings, very tough, with carapace all over. There are almost innumerable different sub-species of vodarak. They can stand anywhere from a couple feet tall to over ten feet depending on the type.

They are quite territorial and very hierarchal, and they have a tight, caste-based system where every type of vodarak has their place. They don't tend to understand other mortal species very easily because of how intricate their eusocial society is. Most vodarak don't reproduce at all: their reproduction is done by stationary and grounded vodarak called queens, who they protect at all costs (these queens are basically [[Xeon#Technology|zoatech]] buildings built for spawning vodarak).

Conflicts with the vodarak began in 1572 AE when the Interstellar Coalition started taking some of their territory and colonizing it. Small skirmishes turn into larger battles and pretty soon the IC and the vodarak are completely hostile to one another. At this point the vodarak have been labeled "the enemy", with endless propaganda to make them seem barbaric and vicious.

They communicate with other vodarak by a combination of complex hormone interactions and connection through the xeon.

## Goal

Depends on the group: the vodarak are not a hive-mind but they're all invested in their species' survival. Some tribes of vodarak are more amenable to peace, most at this point want to violently purge archonic mortals from their lands, and a few want to destroy all archonic mortals completely.

# Locations

From the Dtthida Current, have colonies in a whole bunch of planets all throughout the current. They make spaceships and can warprun but can't portal as of yet so they're confined to the Dtthida Current.

Jiyyagu (a jungle megastructure in the Dtthida Current) is their homeworld, so they're the most populous there.
