# Basic Details

Name pronounced "tor-eh-nah"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: Survival
- Titles: Tentdweller, Farwalker
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Third Eldest
- Usual Gender: Female
- Mortal Species: Torren
- Alternate Forms: just an unassuming traveler of any species, sometimes even an animal; a torren

# Description

Torrena is, above all, *tough*: she's never lived in the Center, the home of the gods, just because that is not her way. 

Her way is to rough it, to create her own shelters and tools, to travel the wilderness in order to understand it and feel close to the universe, and through the eons she has learned deeply what it feels like to live at the edge of survival. 

She prefers to almost always keep herself cut off from her godly powers in order to make her survival a true *challenge*, shifting into all different mortal species so she can see what it feels like to be them.

Torrena's godly form is that of a short humanoid being with torren skin, rough, work-worn hands, dressed in browns and earthy greens, though she only assumes her godly form for very special occasions

Associated with taking the steps necessary to survive in a sometimes brutal and unfair universe, and trying to live life in a comfortable and practical way in spite of it all.

# Personality

Committed, loyal, security-oriented, and responsible. Likes to be alone so isn't often seen around the other Archons; to be honest she much prefers the company of the humble.

She's seen as boring by some of the other Archons, which to her is a small price to pay for her dependability and consistency.

# Powers

She's extremely tough, can make all those around her physically tougher and give them stamina. She's one of the few Archons that's gotten good at transmutation and can create food and water out of nowhere. She has very intense healing powers, and if she needs to she can manipulate the elements.

# Myths and Stories

Stories involving Torrena almost always has her showing up with food during a famine, bringing blankets during the winter, creating water during the hot of summer, or some other humble but impactful act. 

Rarely will she completely take away the source of any pain, as she knows the universe is ultimately uncontrollable and she won't always be around to help her children and other mortals.
