# Basic Details

Name pronounced "eye"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: Wisdom
- Titles: The Allseer, The Patient One
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Eldest
- Usual Gender: Nonbinary
- Mortal Species: Aith
- Alternate Forms: a white, glowing elk-creature.

# Description

The wisest and most senior of the archons, and the best at magic. Specializes in the intersection between xeonics and language, and helped the aith develop their unique xenic language.

Usually appears as an aith or a tall, pale, glowing androgynous humanoid, sometimes with more than two arms or eyes, dressed in soft, draping whites, blues, and greens.

Associated with the rules of dealing with people and one's self in an intelligent and patient way.

# Personality

Rational, idealistic, principled and purposeful, they're the natural leader of the Archons, or at least the symbolic leader.

Pretty reserved, doesn't like to interfere in the dealings of mortals. They live with the aith, who are just as hands-off as Ai is. The other Archons go to them for advice but sometimes secretly resent her for staying so far away.

# Powers

Made their own xenic language that only they and the aith know (language called Aith or Ai-da). This works in a different way than the normal xenic language that's used in most magic: it works with the xeon at a more fundamental level, making it more laborious and time-intensive to use but enabling those who know it to control the elements and wield more power overall.

Their magic has advanced mechanisms for communication, basic physics, time, teleportation, and sight, but doesn't have any fast ways to inflict violence (though given time they can do insane things like control the weather and create volcanoes). Can portal at will.

# Myths and Stories

Most myths including Ai have them imparting wisdom in a dream to a mortal who needs it or speaking through prophets to implore for peace and restraint.
