# Basic Details

Name pronounced "vai-ah-nah"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: Change
- Titles: Lady River
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Fourth Eldest
- Usual Gender: Female
- Mortal Species: [[Humans]], [[Kaldi]]
- Alternate Forms: a shifting, amorphous being; a stream or river that wasn't there before

# Description

Vaiana is the most easy-going of the archons, good at helping people get along and cope with change.

Almost always appears as a human or kaldi of varying shape, size, and color, but usually a woman. In her god form she's a kindly-looking, tall human with dark brown or tan skin and long black hair dressed in intricate and patterned greens, blues, and purples.

Associated with human, social progress and the change that always happens, no matter whether you want it to come or not.

# Personality

Kind, generous, patient, and understanding, sometimes to a fault. Very willing to work with people where they are but is able to make hard decisions when push *really* comes to shove (see [[Basic XRU History#The First Age (40,102-28,989 PAE)|the Valdalik Split]]).

She is *always* working behind the scenes to make things work out for all the archonic species but especially humans and the kaldi, as they are her children.

The other Archons respect her direction but sometimes get impatient with her willingness to let things go.

# Powers

Has and uses all xenic effects (but especially the more whimsical magical ones), but is gentle and doesn't usually use them fiercely. Has a lot of spirit (powerful and otherwise), dragon, and elemental friends that she trades favors with, so her work is sometimes obscured behind an intermediary. Also has some element manipulation powers but doesn't often use them.

# Myths and Stories

There are many myths of Vaiana especially among the humans and the kaldi, most of them simply about her helping random people disguised as a mortal. She's been known to heal the sick and bring peace everywhere she goes, rarely engaging in violence unless she **absolutely** needs to.
