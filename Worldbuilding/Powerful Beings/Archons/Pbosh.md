# Basic Details

Name pronounced "bosh"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: Passion
- Titles: The Boar, The Wrathful
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Fourth Youngest
- Usual Gender: Male
- Mortal Species: [[Pbosh (S)|Pbosh]]
- Alternate Forms: A large boar-creature

# Description

Pbosh is extremely intense, a trait only matched in strength by his generosity and loyalty. He's always searching for mortals with massive dreams who he can support and help them make the world a better place.

His godly form is that of a [[Pbosh (S)|pbosh]] draped in reds and oranges, but he is most often in the form of a massive boar-creature, residing in the forests near the equator of Pbosh Oo'lo with his hoard of immortal compatriots, also in boar form.

Associated with passion and desire, in chasing dreams and loving family and friends.

# Personality

Will always protect what's his! He's the most willing to engage in outright skirmishes against the dark gods so he's the best at warfare among the Archons, his military mind honed by countless battles across the ages.

Often works with [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Osadona|Osadona]] in her fight against evil.

Commonly at odds with [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Rtolka|Rtolka]]. Very exciting, busy, and spontaneous.

# Powers

Has all red xeon powers: his physical strength is unmatched by all except Toko, his older brother. Has a massive flaming spear that he wields in battle to beat down his foes.

He likes to keep things simple, relying on physical strength and fighting mostly like a superpowered [[Warrior|red adept]] with all red powers.

He can read auras and portal at will.

# Myths and Stories

Most stories involving Pbosh have him taking up arms against some evil foe, arming an underdog against a despicable superior force, or inspiring his children and the other archonic species to fight for good.

His grudge against Rtolka has been mostly resolved but was the spark for the inter-species enmity between the pbosh and the rtolka. Pbosh regrets this greatly.
