# Basic Details

Name pronounced "ex-ohn"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: Progress
- Titles: The Technologist
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Middle
- Usual Gender: Male
- Mortal Species: [[Exoziniks]]
- Alternate Forms: A dragon made entirely of technology (metal, wires, gems); an AI in the gray web; a robot of any type

# Description

Exon is the most forward-looking of all the Archons, always planning for the future and trying to figure out where mortal society is going next. He likes to share his inventions with other people in subtle ways, and is always guiding sensitive individuals to important discoveries.

Most often shows up as an exozinik, but will sometimes appear as a being inside an existing robot or an AI ghost in the gray web. He lives on Vilidan Prime, a moon of Exozina made entirely of ferrotech.

Associated with technological progress and innovation, he's the one that creates or encourages the yearning for progress and the solving of problems around the universe.

# Personality

Very success- and results-oriented, as well as adaptive and driven. He can be pretty intense, and is literally always working or experimenting.

Works a lot with Rtolka to build intricate webs of knowledge but sometimes gets on his nerves with Exon's penchant for movement and exploration over documentation.

In the past has had a weakness for pushing himself and his species too hard, or to explore in places they shouldn't.

# Powers

Has all gray xeon powers and transmutation and manipulation of elements, as well as some mind reading and telepathy and teleportation.

Can understand and control all types of technology.

# Myths and Stories

Stories involving Exon almost always have him giving an inventory or explorer the little push they need toward a breakthrough, the tiny idea that pushes them over the edge, the bit of encouragement that makes them believe that they are just on the precipice of something great.
