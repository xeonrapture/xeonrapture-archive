# Basic Details

Name pronounced "ruh-toll-kuh"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: Knowledge
- Titles: The Owl, The Shrewd
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Third Youngest
- Usual Gender: Male
- Mortal Species: [[Rtolka (S)|Rtolka]]
- Alternate Forms: an owl, a fox

# Description

Rtolka is the most detail-oriented and meticulous of all the Archons, having spent millennia writing, reading, collecting, and categorizing records from all across the Isveh Supercluster.

Lives in a soaring tree city-library on Rtolka-Dala, right with his people who see him on occasion. He usually appears as a rtolka clad in black and green, a book in one hand and a magical staff in the other.

Associated with the endless and productive search for knowledge about the universe and the way things work.

# Personality

Very thoughtful and intelligent, and is as good at teaching as he is at parsing new information and interpreting old texts.

Often works with [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Exon|Exon]] on technological breakthroughs and new fields of study. Commonly at odds with [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Pbosh|Pbosh]]. Intense, perceptive, and isolated. 

Can be a little dry and boring at times and even a little socially thoughtless but makes up for it with his meticulousness and breadth of knowledge.

# Powers

Has all gray xenic powers as well as an incredibly deep grasp of the xenic language, with which he wields immense power over the xeon (he can even use it to manipulate the elements, something most magicians can't do).

# Myths and Stories

The majority of stories about Rtolka involve him inspiring some logical breakthrough such as a leap forward in math or physics or the discovery of some new facet of reality through a novel experiment.

His grudge against Pbosh has been mostly resolved but was the spark for the inter-species enmity between the pbosh and the rtolka. Rtolka regrets this greatly.
