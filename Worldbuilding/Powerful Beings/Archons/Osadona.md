# Basic Details

Name pronounced "awe-suh-doh-nuh"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: The Many
- Titles: Hearth Goddess
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Youngest
- Usual Gender: Female
- Mortal Species: Osadona
- Alternate Forms: doesn't really ever take an alternate form

# Description

Osadona is the "youngest" of the Archons and so is often overlooked by the other Archons and even most mortals (just like Gatrathki is), but to ignore Osadona would to be ignore a fundamental and crucial aspect of intelligent existence: community, fellowship, understanding, solidarity.

She's the one that stands up for the little guy, the one that can't stand anyone being left behind. She lives with the osadona on Osa disguised as their immortal head matriarch, and leads them in purging some of the more pernicious evil influences throughout the Isveh Supercluster (such as demons, devils, and the more subtle dark and elder gods).

Her godly form is her matriarch form but younger, and absent any flashy touches most of the other gods include.

Associated with peace and unity in the community.

# Personality

Very intelligent and humble, definitely the most humble of all the Archons (even more than Torrena, who likes to get at least a *little* bit of recognition for what she does).

She's very caring, interested, and generous with everybody in her community and others she comes in contact with, but can also be very possessive.

Her and [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Gatrathki|Gatrathki]] are very close, and she's actually pretty close with [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Torrena|Torrena]] as well.

# Powers

She is a seer, so she can both see into the xeon as well as into the future and the past; she also has all gray and green xenic powers.

Osadona can also portal at will and see into the xeon.

# Myths and Stories

Osadona is subtle enough that most people that are helped by her learn that it was her after the fact and against her will. She's been known to heal, bring people together so they can fight back against their oppressors, and inspire towards unity over division.
