# Basic Details

Name pronounced "gah-trath-kee"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: The One
- Titles: The Lonely God, The Secretive One
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Second Youngest
- Usual Gender: Male
- Mortal Species: None
- Alternate Forms: a light-years wide galactic humanoid form, outlined in white and with a black, star-speckled body

# Description

Gatrathki is an odd one, a god that hasn't made any mortal species and doesn't plan to; he likes being alone with only occasional contact with the other gods and his immortals. He lives in some far-off place that not many know of, and watches the mortals. 

After some observation he chooses humanoids that have a very interesting, individual, and unique mission and style and nurtures them with direction and even ; if they prove worthy he makes them immortals and then maybe lesser gods.

Gatrathki doesn't like to show himself, not even coming to any Archon gatherings, preferring to send an intermediary or just having Osadona tell him what happened afterwards.

Associated with individuality and being one's self wholly and completely.

# Personality

He's sensitive and withdrawn, and can be temperamental, and is kind of self-absorbed in a way, but is also very innovative and interesting for those who know him. He's also inspired in a lot of ways and understands the individual and the personal world very deeply.

Him and [[xr/Worldbuilding/Powerful Beings/Archons/Osadona|Osadona]] are very close; in fact Osadona is Gatrathki's closest and maybe only real friend.

# Powers

His powers are unknown; he might be intervening in mortal affairs but nobody knows about it because he's so subtle and likes not to be seen or known.

# Myths and Stories

He is known through his acolytes, random, unique people who show up saying that Gatrathki has chosen them. Some of these people then try to nurture other people with singular missions, effectively passing Gatrathki's vision along.
