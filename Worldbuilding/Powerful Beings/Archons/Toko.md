# Basic Details

Name pronounced "toh-koh"

- Being Type: Middle God
- Domain: The Law
- Titles: Arm of Justice, Astral Guardian
- Pantheon: Abaian (Archonic)
- Archon Order: Second Eldest
- Usual Gender: Male
- Mortal Species: Tolt
- Alternate Forms: a massive blue dragon; any number of massive, powerful beasts, including wyverns

# Description

Toko is strong, strict, and intense, all traits that make him the perfect person to protect the mortal species from all types of evil.

He's the one to inspire people to organize and uphold just forms of government, as well as judge fairly and wisely. Though he is the God of the Law, he generally understands the role of mercy, and, as such, works with Vaiana in changing and helping people who need it (though he has been known to forget the need for mercy at times, just as Vaiana occasionally forgets the need for justice).

He instructs Kavak and helps him lead the Keepers, a group of humanoid constructs that try to keep the corners of the Isveh Supercluster safe.

# Personality

He's very intense, confident, and willful, and very, very good at protecting the universe.

The other Archons respect him for his strength but are sometimes annoyed by his intensity and stubborness.

# Powers

Toko utilizes force fields, endurance and burst strength, weight manipulation, temperature manipulation, and inertial dampening quite a bit, as well as being being basically indestructible*.

Also has pretty intense element manipulation powers, and can portal at will.

He can make Keepers out of humanoids, can read people's intentions perfectly. Has advanced xeon sight.

\*The other gods would maybe die if you dropped a planet on their head, but Toko would just...walk it off.

# Myths and Stories

Most myths and stories dealing with Toko involve him stopping a natural disaster from destroying a town or city; he is also known to destroy asteroids that would make a mortal species go extinct.

He actively is engaged in beating back the ozokaz and the zvec, as well as other threats the mortal species don't know about.
