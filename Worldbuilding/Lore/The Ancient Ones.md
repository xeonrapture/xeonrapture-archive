# Description

The Ancient Ones are an old species that created massive, powerful stone technology and lasted for thousands of thousands of years before getting completely wiped out somewhere around 10,000 years ago. No one knows what wiped them out, but their space stations and outposts scattered all around the supercluster show scars of war.

Much of today's technology has been inspired by the remnants of their tech, which is a sort of magic-blend geotech that uses runes and gems to channel the xeon. Some of their technology still functions: old robots still whir to life and cause havoc, artifacts are still usable and powerful, shrines glow and emit strange noises.

# The Historical Record

There is only a little bit of consensus about how the Ancient Ones actually were: there are murals depicting them as skinny, hairless beings with large round heads and big, glassy eyes. Some researchers and archeologists say that they were very organized and civilized, others say they were constantly fractured and infighting.

No one has been able to fully decipher what the Ancient One language says, but it was their runes that inspired modern rune usage for rituals, retrotech, and magical artifacts.

# The Ancient One Eras

From observing Ancient One architecture, there are three distinct eras in Ancient One history:

- Dark Era (189,000 PAE-167,400 PAE): more crude structures, more symbolic placement of structures, structures placed on xenic lay lines; ritualistic temples
- High Era (167,400 PAE-98,000 PAE): spaceports, vast cavern cities, plane-oriented gravity, arches and columns
- Late Era (98,000 PAE-85,658 PAE): brutalist, hard lines, sharp corners, more complex gravity structures, more war-focused spaceports and city structure

As you can see, their history was extremely long and storied; the only reasons we have any of their artifacts is because 1) they were constructed very sturdily and 2) much of their architecture was in space and so protected from any terrestrial erosion.

# Ruins Locations

There are lots of ruins on Ydilik and there used to be many on Valda until they were buried and destroyed (some can still be found in the lower strata).

There are some ruins on Voroki, lots of ruins in the tunnel systems of Exozina, and there's a large Ancient One space station called Carnithanth in exozinik space (that still has generated atmosphere).
