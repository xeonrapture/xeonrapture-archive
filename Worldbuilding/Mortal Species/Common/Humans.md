# Basic Details

- Common Chroma: Any
- Rarity: Common
- Native Planet: Valda and Ydilik
- Created By: Vaiana, Goddess of Change
- Creation Date: 51,535 PAE (Pre-Amethyst Age)

# Physical Description

They're humans, and are as varied as humans on Earth are.

Valdan humans can have brightly-colored eyes and hair, and Ydilikian humans have the genetic quirk of getting the eye color of both their parents: mom on the inside and dad on the outside, with the color band width depending on the individual and the genes involved.

# <abbr title="These personality traits are generalizations, and better describe the stereotype about this species than the species' temperament itself. Each mortal species contains a huge range of personalities and motivations.">Personality Traits</abbr>

Vaiana created humans to be generalists so they have a penchant for thinking out of the box, for bursts of creativity, for incorporating many different ideas into novel solutions.

They're always changing and adapting, and they can both be the most generous and understanding and the most depraved and power-hungry. They're generalists in personality as much as they are in physicality.

# Known For

Humans are known for so much: most of the consumer tech around the Isveh Supercluster is manufactured by corps from Valda, as is a large amount of magic items and artifacts.

Humans have the most control and are definitely the most oppressive species around the supercluster (Valdan corps like Krangor and Ailith, Valdan gangs like Lyon and Strobe, Ydilikian governments like the kingdoms of Eyyendal and Vyria, and a Ydilikian religion called Vaiana's heart are all ever-present and overbearing).

They've also done the most to connect all the mortal species than any other, given that they invented portals and then used them to spread their influence. They also have the most colonies around the supercluster by far.

# History

General history around the Isveh Supercluster tends to favor humans because of how influential they've been, to the point that the three ages are based on important things that happened to humans (the First Age starts when humans make xenic items in 40,102 PAE, the Second Age starts when they discover magic in 28,929 PAE, the Third Age starts when they make the first intergalactic portal in 894 AE).

- Age of Creation (70,000-40,102 PAE)
	- Humans created (51,535 PAE)
	- Humans get xenic powers (48,568 PAE)
- First Age (40,102-28,989 PAE)
	- Valdalik War (32416-31534 PAE) and the planet Valdalik is split into Valda and Ydilik by Vaiana
	    - Humans were on the verge of destroying themselves until Vaiana stepped in
- Second Age (28,989 PAE-894 AE)
	- Humans discover silver and gold xenic powers (28,989 PAE)
	- Humans discover diamond xenic power (11,539 PAE)
	- Diothca War on Valda (10,416-9,405 PAE)
	- Humans discover scrying (6,997 PAE)
	- Humans discover steel xenic tech, enabling force fields (5,071 PAE)
	- First Motic War (4,388-4,084 PAE)
	- Second Motic War (2,671-1,823 PAE)
	- Battle of Gjgrathka (363 PAE)
	- First Amethyst Portal (1 AE)
- Third Age (894 AE-Today)
	- First intergalactic portal (894 AE)
	- First inter-current portal connecting Toloi Current with Ghashnalah Current (1,300 AE)
	- Spirit anchors invented (1,325 AE)
	- Corruption on Valda and Ydilik grows (around 1,375 AE)
