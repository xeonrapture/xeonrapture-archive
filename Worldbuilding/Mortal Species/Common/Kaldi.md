# Basic Details

Name pronounced "cahl-dee"

- Common Chroma: Green
- Rarity: Common
- Native Planet: Kaldi
- Created By: Vaiana, Goddess of Change
- Creation Date: 43,885 PAE (Pre-Amethyst Age)

# Physical Description

More or less like humans, but with very richly-colored skin (like from dark blue or green to bright, vivid colors). Vibrant, colorful eyes, long slender tails (sometimes with fur), pointed ears.

They can hear way lower and higher than humans can, and have two vocal chords, so they can double up their words in pitch.

Bisexuality is much more common among kaldi than other mortal species.

# <abbr title="These personality traits are generalizations, and better describe the stereotype about this species than the species' temperament itself. Each mortal species contains a huge range of personalities and motivations.">Personality Traits</abbr>

All about self-expression, and even when they're in a less expressive field they make a ton of time to express themselves in some way. Some of them are more reserved, for sure, but the vast majority of kaldi have this need to express.

Graceful and passionate, highly invested in dance and the arts, very emotionally intelligent.

They can be very manipulative and controlling at worst, or very supportive and gentle at best.

# Known For

The kaldi are known for their joyous and beautiful celebrations and get-togethers, where they sing and dance and do ceremonies. They generally don't care about technology, but are extremely invested in the preservation and exploration of culture and cultural history.

Their civilization has never been very technologically advanced, but they live fully and are deep into art and philosophy. Can be very good diplomats if they exercise their empathy and social acumen correctly.

Their language is words and music glued together; it's tonal and melodic like no other language is, and as such their music is lush and beautiful.
# History

They prefer to not physically record history or their stories, poems, and songs, even though they're the most beautiful in the universe. They like things to be transient and for each person who learns them to change them for themselves, so they heavily emphasize a vocally-transmitted history tradition. That way, their culture is never stagnant and is always reflecting the state of the kaldi people themselves.
