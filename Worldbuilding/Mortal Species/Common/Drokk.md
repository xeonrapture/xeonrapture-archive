# Basic Details

- Common Chroma: Blue
- Rarity: Common
- Native Planet: Ydilik
- Created By: Kronkkar, God of the Drokk
- Creation Date: 5,646,200 PAE (Pre-Amethyst Age)

# Physical Description

Basically just look like dwarves: short, stocky bodies, often very hairy, strong and tough.

# <abbr title="These personality traits are generalizations, and better describe the stereotype about this species than the species' temperament itself. Each mortal species contains a huge range of personalities and motivations.">Personality Traits</abbr>

Very hardy, tough and dependable. Often have very tight familial relationships, keeping careful note of their ancestors names for generations and generations.

At their worst they're incredibly stubborn, unable to take no for an answer, obsessed with material things and precious substances.

# Known For

On Ydilik, their homeworld, the drokk are known for sturdy magical items, armor, and weapons made with the most precious of metals and gems. They run extensive mining operations in most of the mountain ranges of Ydilik, delving deep into the earth in search of precious substances. They traditionally stay away from the magics of humans and rtolka, preferring to rely on their craftsmanship (though they do have their fair share of alchemists).

Outside of Ydilik the drokk are generally known for their heavy, dependable technology, especially for mining; they tend to gravitate toward technology, whether or not there's any restrictions on magic or chip technology in the place that they live.

# History

The drokk were created around 5,646,200 PAE.

It is generally unknown how exactly the drokk were created, especially according to most humans: as far as Ydilikians know, the drokk were always there, in the mountains of Ydilik, working their craft, carving and shaping stone, drinking their mead.

As far as specific historical events goes, check out [[Humans#History|human history]], as everything that happened to them on Ydilik happened (more or less) to the drokk.
