# Basic Details

Name pronounced "bosh"

- Common Chroma: Red
- Rarity: Common
- Native Planet: Pbosh Oo'lo
- Created By: Pbosh
- Creation Date: 49,665 PAE (Pre-Amethyst Age)

# Physical Description

Thick and stocky, hairless, dark blue and brown skin with six gold or brown eyes, two main ones and four smaller ones. Four fingers per hand. Adults are 6-8 ft tall, and just about as wide. Usually quite chubby, also usually extremely strong.

Look kinda like [this guy](https://disney.fandom.com/wiki/Jumba_Jookiba).

# <abbr title="These personality traits are generalizations, and better describe the stereotype about this species than the species' temperament itself. Each mortal species contains a huge range of personalities and motivations.">Personality Traits</abbr>

The pbosh's inclination is to follow their passions, protect and care for the people that they love, and build beautiful heritages for themselves and their children.

Can be monarchical, intense, territorial at worst, and passionate, generous, and brave at best. Pretty low fertility rate so they aren't quite as numerous as other common species, but they're *very* hardy so they thrive in the gnarliest of places, and their culture is very inter-generational so they keep things lively.

# Known For

The pbosh's technology is a very sturdy, environmentally-resistant, magitech-y form of ferrotech; their homeworld is probably the most hostile of all the common species so they know how to survive in tough environments.

Their culture includes a very inclusive generosity, so most places they are they're willing to take care of those that need it (unless you run into Gondo Nation fanatics, in which case their fascist nutjobs).

The pbosh are on the front lines of the fight against bigotry and evil pretty much everywhere you go.

# History

Their monarch have traditionally been worshipped as a physical manifestation of Pbosh himself, and they've had a connection with Pbosh and been able to speak with him and do his will throughout the ages (Pbosh is one of the more involved of the Archons).

made their version of ferrotech in 32933 PAE, had a massive war with the rtolka from 27232 PAE to 22305 PAE and another from 795 AE to 800 AE. discovered warprunning in 8415 PAE

joined the Interstellar Coalition in 1305
