# Basic Details

Name pronounced "ex-oh-zee-nicks"

- Common Chroma: Gray
- Rarity: Common
- Native Planet: Exozina
- Created By: Exon, God of Progress
- Creation Date: 54,506 PAE (Pre-Amethyst Age)

# Physical Description

An exozinik has black to light gray skin (always grayscale), hairless, with chrome, sometimes-colored metal strands for hair. Their hands and feet are covered with a shiny metal skin that creeps up their arms and legs and breaks up into their normal skin halfway up their calves and forearms; the same metal skin runs up their spine and seeps a bit into their back around the spine and up their neck into the back of their head.

They have double-jointed legs, and their silicon-based biology necessitates a unique type of food. Irises are colored chrome, and they have shimmery metallic blue blood.

# <abbr title="These personality traits are generalizations, and better describe the stereotype about this species than the species' temperament itself. Each mortal species contains a huge range of personalities and motivations.">Personality Traits</abbr>

Really into exploration, and they tend to be really curious, both in intellectual fields and in discovering the world. Inventive and resourceful, the exoziniks were the first mortal species to create chip technology, leave their planet, and discover warprunning.

At their worst they will stop at nothing for progress, even destroying important, dependable institutions and infrastructure to make them anew.

# Known For

Exoziniks are known for a really unique type of ferrotech where they use xeon binding to bend super-heated metal into very unnatural shapes; most of their tech features xeon binding extensively, and they have a huge culture of artisan crafting over any sort of mass production, so most of what they make is high quality.

They're also always exploring every corner of the universe, even places that should be left alone, and pushing science to its absolute limits, even sometimes into danger.
