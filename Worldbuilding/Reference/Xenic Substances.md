# Intro to Xenic Substances

There are a large amount of substances, both gems and metals, that produce strange effects when the xeon is channeled through them. There are multiple ways to channel xeon through a substance, including through magic, technology, or channeling xeon into them manually through your body.

## Common Substances and Technology

In general, technology can't grant a person powers, it's constructed to make the effect themselves. For example, a tech object with silver in it won't really grant a person endurance strength and durability, but maybe it will make the *object itself* more durable. So animal shape and influence emotion and a couple others are also off-limits from technology.
