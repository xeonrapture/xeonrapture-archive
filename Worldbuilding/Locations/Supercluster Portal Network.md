# Interstellar Lay Line Portals

There are many interstellar lay line portals connecting all sorts of planets with one another and connecting all sorts of places to the [[xr/Worldbuilding/Lore/The Xenic Plane|xenic plane]], but nobody has ever really been able to reliably transport large amounts of materials through these portals because they're much too rare, with too long of a time period between instances of them opening, to be dependable.

Because that's how lay line portals almost always work: they either pop up out of nowhere, with very subtle or no warning at all, or they show up periodically, like every two years, or three times in a row and then not again for a hundred years, or even up to thousands of years between appearances.

They appear due to xenic lay lines crossing, with strong blue streams tangling up with strong red streams and creating pools of purple xeon that turn into natural portals.

Portals that connect two physical places are *extremely* rare, much more rare than portals that open directly into the xenic plane (though those are rare too).

There are three types of lay line portals:
- Seasonal-->these portals' periods are tied to the [[Xeon#The Xenic Seasons|xenic season]], which [lasts an Earth year](https://app.xeonrapture.com/xr-tools/xr-clock.html) and whose seasons are in line with those of the northern hemisphere
- Short Period-->these portals have periods under 200 years
- Long Period-->these portals have period over 200 years

# Commercial Mortal-Made Portals

There are several types of mortal-made portals:
- Personal/Small
	- The *extremely* rich sometimes have their own portals
		- Some even have a web of portal machines that interconnect
		- High-up Krangor officials have a series they use
	- Can also be created as a ritual by a bunch of high-level magicians
- Commercial
	- Tech or magic portals that are maintained in webs connected to other high traffic areas
	- These often require payment to use, though some places have them as a public service
- Industrial
	- Heavy, high-tech (or high-magic) portals used to transport high volumes of materials
	- **Cannot be used to transport reactive xenic materials!** These materials must be warprun

The bigger and more stable the portal the more purple xeon you'll need! The most advanced and biggest portals use celestite to transfer massive amounts of xeon across space instantaneously, sometimes farming the red from spaceships right next to stars or even taking purple xeon right from a black hole!

Most portals have to have pure blue and red warprun in, or (more rarely) generate blue and red right there. Once they have enough blue and red they can combine them into purple and use it to create or sustain the portal.

When not in use portals will usually be reduced down to portal seeds, tiny but stable and connected portals that are basically unusable until more purple is pumped in. It takes much more xeon to start up a portal from nothing, so they keep the portals technically open but small, like pilot lights.
