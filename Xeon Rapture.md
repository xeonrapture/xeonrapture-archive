%% this spiel is meant to give you a top-down view of xr worldbuilding, saying what sets it apart from other fictional worlds, and making it feel mysterious, expansive, and interesting %%

The Isveh Supercluster is an astral star structure more than a billion light years wide formed in the shape of a three-dimensional star, it's twelve arms flowing like rivers in all directions.

For hundreds of billions of years an innumerable variety of plants and animals have evolved and gone extinct, mortal people of all different species have flourished and self-extinguished, kings and presidents have ruled and gods have been created and killed.

But the time is now, and the year is 1666 of the Amethyst Age. Portals connect the farthest reaches of space, colonies filled with every mortal intelligent species spread to the tip of every starcurrent and every tributary, and humans of the twin planets Valda and Ydilik stage proxy wars all across the supercluster.

Throughout all the magics of the rtolka and the high technology of the exoziniks runs the xeon, the fundamental spiritual and metaphysical substance of reality, the immaterial matter that souls are made of, the energy the gods use to create and destroy. 

When charged it is bright and vivid and comes in all colors, all made from a foundation of four: gray, green, red, and blue.

Gray xeon is rarely visible and everywhere, touching outer space and the place between things, thinking slowly, ponderously, dividing and subdividing and collecting bits of itself into shards of understanding.

Green xeon arises with life and the living, and it breathes and lives with them. It flourishes in the wildest places, in the depths of nebulas and on jungle planets, and it grows and helps grow.

Red xeon is the heat, the passion that strikes through it all, the stars, burning bright and fueling the universe.

Blue xeon is the cold and the steady ground, the law and binding of gravity, the planets and all solid things.

Throughout the ages mortals have learned to tame the xeon in a multitude of ways, channeling it through their bodies and spirits to give themselves superpowers, harnessing it through words and gestures as magic or through circuits and chips as technology. Mortals have even learned to deal with the spirits and the gods, make pacts with them, and work with them to achieve their ends.

Enter a massive world, where dark gods scheme and corporations plunder, wyverns devastate and a multitude of intelligent mortal species war and ravage, build and collaborate. There's always something new to discover, and an adventure to be had, in the Xeon Rapture Universe.

Welcome to the Xeon Rapture Universe!

The XRU is a colorful fictional world built for vivid physics-bending anime fights. It's a supercluster-sprawling sci-fantasy blend with a magic system that harnesses the same spiritual substance that the technology does.

The Xeon Rapture Universe is designed as a work of collaborative storytelling, where everybody who participates in it can shape its canon and worldbuilding [in real time](https://app.xeonrapture.com/xr-tools/xr-clock.html). Join me in building it out and bringing it to life!

This is the home for all of the rules and worldbuilding info for the Xeon Rapture Universe and the Xeon Rapture RPG.
