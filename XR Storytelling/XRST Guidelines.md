# Canon

When you tell stories in the XRU you have a couple of different options for dealing with canon:
1. XR Canon: Abide by a few guidelines and your stories will be considered part of the XR Canon! These rules limit your stories a bit but make it so that your stories don't break other people's stories.
2. XR AU: Take the XRU and make it your own by creating an XR AU (Alternate Universe) where you can do anything you want, break any rule, ship any characters, destroy any planet, kill the gods, etc.

When you make XR content, try to label it with either XR Canon or XR AU.

If you do decide to go the XR Canon route, here are some guidelines:

| ENCOURAGED                                                                                                                                                                                 | OFF LIMITS                                                                                                                                                                              |
| :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Making up locations, people, events, and features in places that already exist                                                                                                             | Varying widely from descriptions of already-existing places                                                                                                                             |
| Making up all sorts of locations all around the supercluster (mostly colonies and places to explore)                                                                                       | Breaking the status quo for large, established areas                                                                                                                                    |
| Disturbing the status quo for smaller areas and for places you make up                                                                                                                     | Making up big established locations that'd affect the economic landscape of the supercluster                                                                                            |
| Making and killing all sorts of characters, including immortals and local gods                                                                                                             | Killing canon NPCs or gods                                                                                                                                                              |
| Making all sorts of power combinations and even new powers                                                                                                                                 | Breaking the rules of how xeon works                                                                                                                                                    |
| Making powerful characters!                                                                                                                                                                | Making characters them so powerful that they can beat an immortal one-on-one                                                                                                            |
| Depicting violence, sexuality, horror, bigotry, or anything distasteful in an edifying way (e.g. in a way that intends to explore the human condition)                                     | Depicting anything overly or gratuitously degrading, disgusting, or horrifying, especially with the intent to alienate or offend<br>Encouraging bigotry or extreme anti-social behavior |
| Writing your own stories or making your own music, art, or content of any kind<br>Being influenced by others' work<br>Using other peoples' settings and characters *with their permission* | Stealing from others or passing others' work off as your own<br>Using AI for anything other than coding                                                                                 |

> [!NOTE] Reminder
> And, last but not least, **don't (overly) police other people's stories!**
> 
> Everybody will make mistakes and accidentally break canon: that's ok! It's still canon.
> 
> The XR "canon" is going to be a little loose; we gotta lean into that! Let the canon be a little messy, and give others grace if they break rules or mess stuff up.
>
> If someone else's stories break yours, don't sweat it! Both stories are canon.

The XRU is designed to be so big that everybody has room to play without stepping on anybody else's toes.

So make content! Get creative! Collaborate with others! Try things that may not work!

# Modularity

The XRU is designed to be modular: feel free to only include the parts of the universe that you want to! The XRU includes a massive wealth of different ideas and genres for this purpose, to give you a large amount of things to choose from. Don't feel like you need to explore all the rules or include all the elements in one story or even one series of stories.

For example, most cyberpunk stories probably won't include the gods at all, and a lot of fantasy stories may not include vampires. It all depends on what you want; make the XRU your own playground!

# Power Level Rarity

People with a really high power and experience level are much more rare than those with lower power and experience because it's much, much harder to improve the higher up you go in character level. 

Below is a handy chart that shows the rarity of certain character levels (for reference, there are about 100 trillion people across the whole XRU). See character level chart [[XRST Basic Info#4) Character Level|here]].

| Char.<br>Level<br>Range | Rarity                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0                       | Just a normal person; extremely common, most people are at level 0.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| 1-3                     | A little more rare but not excessively, people in this range are normal adventurers and operatives. They may have a fairly high level of power and skill in their xenic abilities (esp. near level 3) but their experience does not match their power level.<br>A group of people at the top of this power level working together can take on a strong, elite-tier enemy together.<br>About 1 in 1,000 people. |
| 4-6                     | Pretty dang rare, people in this range are quite powerful and are becoming very experienced not just in their xenic powers or their specific fields but in operating in the world at large.<br>A group of people at the top of this power level working together can take on an immortal or an immortal-level elite enemy (such as a wyvern or mech) together.<br>About 1 in 1,000,000 people.                 |
| 7-9                     | Super rare, people in this range are extremely powerful and extremely experienced in the world at large.<br>A group of people at the top of this power level working together can take on a dragon or lesser god together.<br>About 1 in 1,000,000,000 people.                                                                                                                                                 |
| 10+                     | People level 10 and above are vanishingly rare. These people never can quite get to immortal-level power on their own (unless they become an immortal themselves), but they continue to gain power and experience.                                                                                                                                                                                             |
