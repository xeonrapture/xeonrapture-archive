Following are some general guidelines to creating cool, good XRST characters!

- Don't include too many elements, abilities, vibes, or motivations; keep the character's vibe focused.
- Add complexity with contrasting character traits, foibles, eccentricities, and viewpoints, not by stacking too many disparate elements but by making all these character traits parts of a whole. Regular people contain seemingly incompatible traits; good characters often do as well.
- Give them strong motivations! Doesn't even need to be typical or heroic motivations like "avenge my father" or "save my child", can be something quirky or odd but as long as they're passionate about it and will chase after it you can center your stories around that drive.
- Give their backstory a couple of defining moments that make them who they are and that you can reveal in flashbacks to better explain your character.
- Give them things that they hide from themselves and others! Wants they're ashamed of, inclinations they despise, fears for their future they run away from.
