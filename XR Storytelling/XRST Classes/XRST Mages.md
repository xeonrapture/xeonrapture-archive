# Cleric

You've made a pact with a light god or other powerful light entity! Impressive, congrats. Choose a god from the entity list or pick an immortal or dragon.

Often clerics need to go down some good path in order to gain their heavenly power. Would the god you made a pact with require a daily ritual, a general walk of life, or a certain amount of good deeds per week? They might be lenient or strict, but that's up to you.

You may always pray to your god to ask them for guidance or aid, which they may or may not answer given their nature or the type of request. Be careful not to have your god solve your character's problems for them! Sometimes all they need is guidance.

Very few warlocks are good, as dark entities often require some act of great evil in order to gain their power, or commit to some evil path. On the other hand, you might have a goal in common with them. Determine what your god requires, and what they might be driving you towards.

# Sorcerer

Sorcerers are people who have made a pact or somehow have a connection to a devil.

Devils are spirits that arise because of strong emotion or an intense event, whether good or bad. They form from the ambient xeon, and as the xeon starts reacting to the emotions of the mortals that surround the event or location, it starts forming into a spirit. It can then grow in intelligence, learning from the xeon around it and mortals it comes in contact with.

Devils can get powerful enough to be able to interact with the physical world. They can help or hurt, destroy or fabricate.

Some devils get intelligent enough that they can enter pacts with mortals. Some devils (often called wraiths) attach to a mortal early on and start following them around. These devils can then grant the mortal some of their powers, and in return the devils often gain power and intelligence just from being around their mortal and interacting with the mortal realm.

## Making a Devil

The sorcerer class is unique in that it's extremely customizable. Below is the mechanical framework for how it will work, but you get to create a devil that ties into your backstory. Here are some guidelines:

- They can look humanoid or they can look the farthest thing away from humanoid as there is, but their form _has_ to be informed by the event or emotions that formed them.
- They can be evil, they can be good, but more often they're neutral; their goals are formed by the event or emotion that formed them.
- Many devils will enter a pact in exchange for a sacrifice from a mortal. This can be something physical like a finger, an eye, or even an appendage; it can be ethereal like requiring you to do a certain ritual or routine every day to feed them or take care of them; and it can even be something metaphysical like years off of your life. Sometimes devils will enter pacts for no apparent reason! Work with your gm to figure out why your devil would enter a pact, whether formally or informally.
- When in doubt look up Jujutsu Kaisen or Chainsaw Man devil designs.

## Powers

At lvl 1 Sorcerer you pick two of the four abilities below. Theme them to match your devil and your powers! And make sure to pick a Devil Type below for additional skills and buffs.

1. Single-target close attack
2. Single-target ranged attack
3. AOE attack
	1. If you have the ranged attack you can send the AOE somewhere else, otherwise it's centered on yourself. In this case it won't hurt you, but it will hurt anybody who's around you.
4. Single-target Status Effect
	1. Pick one of the Status Effects from the chart below to inflict upon an enemy
	2. Use the ranged attack distance for the range of your status effect (you don't need to have the ranged attack to do a ranged status effect).
	3. You may only use each Status Effect up to 3 times per day.

Here's how you level up:

# Demititan

You've made a pact with a titan. Hmmmmmm, iiiinteresting. Choose a titan from the entity list or make your own (titans coming soon).

Your eyes turn a solid color, with no whites, pupil, or iris.

You can now transmute and morph objects and substances! Including your body!

Demititans can change elements and objects into different shapes and other elements. They can change the element of their own body and then change a small amount of matter outside their body into that element as well through touch.

There is no ranged transmuting! You have to be touching something to transmute it.

Feel free to pick an aesthetic theme for when you morph into whatever element!

## Empowered Immaterial Mode

When you hit Level 12 you may now attack, cast spells, and do abilities while you're immaterial! But as soon as you attack, cast a spell, or do an ability you become half-material for 10 minutes, taking half damage from all sources.

## Types of Substances

Non-transmutables are substances and objects that can't be transmuted unless you spend a large amount of time and effort transmuting them. Semi-transmutables are substances and objects that take 10x the time to transmute because of the substance or the stresses they're under.

Non-transmutables:

- gems
- protected or shielded objects (with tech or magic)
- processed materials (plastics, rubbers)
- strong magic or tech items
- the body of any living being besides you (plants don't count)

Semi-transmutables:

- objects and materials under a large amount of strain (foundations of a building, support struts of a spaceship)
