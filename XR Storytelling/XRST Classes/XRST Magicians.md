# Physic

Physics have learned the xenic language and as a result can tell the xeon what to do!

Physics have focuses that they cast their magic through! It can be pretty much anything though, from a wand to a staff to an orb.

Max Concentration means how many spells you can have up at the same time and still cast other spells. Concentration spells are ones that can be kept up for a period of time.

Any time you level up you can replace 1 of your known spells with a new one.

For range, take the Basic Range above and plug it into "r" in the table.
For the AOE radius, take the half of Basic Range (unless stated otherwise).
0 range is a touch spell.

To start, take 9 Tier I spells from the list below.

Pick a spellcasting style:

- Chroma Generalist: spells are more powerful if the previously-used spell was of the opposite color (red and blue are opposites, green and gray are opposites)
- Chroma Specialist: spells are more powerful if the previously-used spell was of the same color
- AOE Caster: spells are more powerful if the spell is an AOE
- Single-Target Caster: spells are more powerful if the spell has one target

# Possessor

Possessors are very similar to physics but without a lot of the spells that inflict a condition. They get their power from harnessing the spirits of the mortal dead, and as such their magic is taboo and often corrupts the user. It's also more powerful than standard physic magic, and possessors have access to some spookier spells.

Really the only way to be a possessor without becoming evil is to develop relationships with the spirits you've chosen to control, or even better, make relationships with the spirits first and then make pacts with them so that they get something out of the deal.

# Bard

Bards are the fun cousins of the Physics: they manipulate the xeon with words like physics do, but they add song and sometimes dance or visuals to manipulate people's minds. This magic was developed by the kaldi and has been used to motivate groups of people, pump up parties, extract information, and terrify enemies.

Bards sing and an illusion is created in the mind of their target! The bard can manipulate this illusion to achieve a certain effect. These illusions can merge seamlessly with the real world or, if the bard is powerful enough, completely overwhelm every sense.

Pick an instrument or artform! This is what you're good at as a bard and is how you cast your spells! As you level up in bard you get better and better at your chosen artform.

All bards can passively change the emotions of the room when they play music, tell stories, dance, or whatever their art of choice is! The listeners can reject the emotions at will if they desire, as the bard is not forcing these emotions, merely suggesting them.
