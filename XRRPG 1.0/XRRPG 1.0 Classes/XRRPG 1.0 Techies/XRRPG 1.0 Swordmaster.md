# General

You gain a +2 bonus to all skill checks with technology (called the "tech bonus").

You are able to create any of the gadgets you use in this class as long as you can find scrap to make them (you have a +2 bonus to finding scrap). Otherwise you might have to buy parts, which will cost 30 Valdan credits per gadget.

Using a gadget always costs at least the amount of stamina of it's tier (unless they're performing the ability of a lower-level gadget). For example, some gadgets are upgraded versions of lower-level gadgets, and with those devices they can perform the abilities of their lower-level counterparts.

Every time you do an attack that does both dmg and a status effect, the enemy defends with their DDC and then rolls a save against the same attack roll to defend against the status effect.

If you decide to take another Technologist class at any point then you'll have to level it up from 1 to 6.

# Specific Info

You can now make tech melee swords!

Wit/Int swords are thin and lithe and Vig/End swords are beefy, but all the damage they do is the same.

(Essentially, though, the swords you make can be of any size or shape as long as 1) they don't get in the way of an adventure {i.e. no INCOMPREHENSIBLY MASSIVE swords} and 2) they don't change the mechanics at all. The aesthetics are all up to you.)

You may throw your swords if you wish! They deal a [[Damage Dice Tier|dice tier]] less of damage when you use them this way.

You gain the ability to make 3 powerful attacks per day, as detailed below. From lvl 2 on these powerful attacks can be ranged but can only ever target one person. If you miss a powerful attack you don't expend a use of it. Ranged and AOE powerful attacks do a [[Damage Dice Tier|dice tier]] less of damage.

Below are sword types you can choose from, as well as some Sword Modifiers that you attach to a sword to give it passive benefits. Each Sword Modifier can only be attached to one of your swords, including swords you buy or find.

Any of the extra sword effects can be executed with a ranged attack if the effect is put onto a powerful attack.
