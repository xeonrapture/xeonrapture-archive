You've made a pact with a light god or other powerful light entity! Impressive, congrats. Choose a god from the entity list or pick an immortal or dragon.

Often clerics need to go down some good path in order to gain their heavenly power. Work with your gm to figure out whether the god you entered into a pact with would require a daily ritual, a general walk of life, or a certain amount of good deeds per week. They might be lenient or strict.

You may always pray to your god to ask them for guidance or aid! The DC will be high and what happens will always up to the GMs discretion.

# General

First, pick an entity to make a pact with! You then are granted a special skill associated with that god: work with your GM to figure out what that should be. The special skill is a plus equal to your Cleric Level: at level 1 it's a +1 skill and at level 6 it's a +6 skill. This skill should be specific and shouldn't have anything to do with combat!

Next, give yourself the Tier 1 ability below; you will get a new ability every time you level up Cleric.

These are all Archonic gods. Eosian Gods will just give you elemental spirits to work with, they won't make pacts with you themselves.

If you'd like to come up with an immortal to make a pact with, feel free to work with your DM to nail down the mechanics.
