Stat: Any

Complexity: Low

You've made a pact with a dark god or other dark entity! Ooooh, spoooooky. Choose a god from the entity list or pick a dark immortal, evil dragon, or demon.

Very few warlocks are good, as dark entities often require some act of great evil in order to gain their power, or commit to some evil path. On the other hand, you might have a goal in common with them. If you want to make a good character that is a warlock, talk to your gm and work something out.

You may always pray to your god to ask them for guidance or aid! The DC will be high and what happens will always up to the GMs discretion, but the odds that your god will give you a rigged deal are extremely high.

# General

First, pick an entity to make a pact with! You then are granted a special skill associated with that god: work with your GM to figure out what that should be. The special skill is a plus equal to your Warlock Level: at level 1 it's a +1 skill and at level 6 it's a +6 skill. This skill should be specific and shouldn't have anything to do with combat!

Next, give yourself the lvl 1 ability below; you will get a new ability every time you level up Warlock.

- Menonic Smite: the attack that originally had a d10 dmg die now has a d12 dmg die, or a d6 becomes a d8.

Last, give yourself the ability that corresponds to the god you picked below! If you picked an immortal, work with your GM to make an ability you can use or pick one of the ones below.

NOTE: If you ever take yourself to 0 HP by using a Warlock skill you are inflicted with the [[Critical Injuries#Necrosis|Necrosis]] Critical Injury.
