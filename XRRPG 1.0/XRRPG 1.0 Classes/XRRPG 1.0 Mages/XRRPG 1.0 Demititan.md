# General Info

You can now transmute and morph objects and substances! Including your body!

Demititans can change elements and objects into different shapes and other elements. They can change the element of their own body and then change a small amount of matter outside their body into that element as well through touch.

There is no ranged transmuting! You have to be touching something to transmute it.

Unless stated otherwise, your ranged attacks (e.g. converting material to your element and throwing it) does a d6 + stat of dmg per stam.

Feel free to pick an aesthetic theme for when you morph into whatever element!

## Empowered Immaterial Mode

When you hit Level 12 you may now attack, cast spells, and do abilities while you're immaterial! But as soon as you attack, cast a spell, or do an ability you become half-material for 10 minutes, taking half damage from all sources.

# Types of Substances

Non-transmutables are substances and objects that can't be transmuted unless you spend a large amount of time and effort transmuting them. Semi-transmutables are substances and objects that take 10x the time to transmute because of the substance or the stresses they're under.

Non-transmutables:

- gems
- protected or shielded objects (with tech or magic)
- processed materials (plastics, rubbers)
- strong magic or tech items
- the body of any living being besides you (plants don't count)

Semi-transmutables:

- objects and materials under a large amount of strain (foundations of a building, support struts of a spaceship)
