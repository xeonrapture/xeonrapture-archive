# General

Bards are the fun cousins of the Physics: they manipulate the xeon with words like physics do, but they add song and sometimes dance or visuals to manipulate people's minds. This magic was developed by the kaldi and has been used to motivate groups of people, pump up parties, extract information, and terrify enemies.

Bards sing and an illusion is created in the mind of their target! The bard can manipulate this illusion to achieve a certain effect. These illusions can merge seamlessly with the real world or, if the bard is powerful enough, completely overwhelm every sense.

Pick an instrument or artform! This is what you're good at as a bard and is how you cast your spells! And you get an extra plus (called your Artistry Plus) when you do regular non-combat checks with this art form.

All bards can passively change the emotions of the room when they play music, tell stories, dance, or whatever their art of choice is! There is no check for this, but the listeners can reject the emotions at will if they desire.

You can use as much stamina as you'd like on any spell, but **you must use at least as much stamina as the spell's level**.

To start, take 4 Tier I spells from the list below.

# Spell List

Unless stated otherwise, spells that require concentration _do not_ require you to continue spending stamina on them each turn.

All bard effects are psychic and bypass xenic and physical defenses!
