## Stamina Leveling

You can spend extra stamina to do extra damage on any attack, and the **Overcharge Limit** is the most stamina you can spend on any one attack.

Exhaustion limit is how much total stamina you can spend before you become exhausted! See [[XRRPG 1.0 Core Rules#Exhaustion]] for more deets.

# Key

## Gaining Health

Each time you level up into a class, you roll their health die and gain that much max health (min of 1).

In addition, every level you gain max health equal to your Endurance stat. This begins at level 1. Whenever you level up your Endurance stat, you gain health equal to your current level (to bring you up to speed).

## Proficiency Die

**Use your Proficiency Die whenever you use a class ability**, including if you're attacking with your class abilities. Damage bonuses are determined per class and sometimes per ability.

## Skills and Stats

You also gain extra skills, bonuses to existing skills, and a +1 stat bonus every so often. **Each stat has a cap of +5!** Skills also have a cap of +5.

When you gain a skill it can be a +2 general skill or a +3 specific skill.

## Powerful Item Limit

This is the limit of how many cybermods you can control to at a time! You only have enough strength to control so many powerful items at the same time.

Clears get +2 to whatever is listed here! Cyberports and other very basic cybermods don't count towards this limit.
