# Dice Limits per Level

Below is a chart giving about how certain Action DCs should feel based on your level and what you're rolling with (extra Mastery Dice, as a Group Action, etc.).

Key:
- MD--> Mastery Dice
- GA--> Group Action
- Lower Limit--> roll feels incredibly easy (success ~85% of the time)
- Comfy--> a roll you hit more than 50% of the time
- Upper Limit--> hard roll (success ~15% of the time)
- Cap--> a crit, the mathematical max you can roll (from 0.25% chance, or 1 out of 400, to ~0.004% chance, or 1 out of 25,000)
