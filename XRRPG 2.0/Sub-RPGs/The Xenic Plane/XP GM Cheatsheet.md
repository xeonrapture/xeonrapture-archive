# Belief

If char has spirit decay divide level of spirit decay by 2 and subtract that *as well as* the -1 when you roll Doubt.

| Roll | Meaning     | Resulting<br>Belief |
| ---- | ----------- | ------------------- |
| 1    | Doubt       | -1                  |
| 2-4  | Expectation | 0                   |
| 5-7  | Hope        | +1                  |
| 8    | Confidence  | +2                  |

# Belief Results

| Belief<br>Rolled | Believability of Act | Meaning                                                                                                                                                            |
| ---------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| ≤-1              | Mundane*             | Outcome is worse than expected, a fumble; whoever did this must've been overcome with uncertainty and gotten into their own head (or inexperienced to begin with). |
| 0                | Reality              | Business as usual. What you would expect the waking world to feel like if no one had xenic powers.                                                                 |
| 1-2              | Strange              | If this happened in the real world you'd swear there was something magical was going on.                                                                           |
| 3-4              | Bizarre              | Very weird. In reality this would be *impossible* without a xenic explanation.                                                                                     |
| 5-6              | Supernatural         | Something only someone very practiced in their xenic powers could pull off.                                                                                        |
| 7-8              | Implausible          | Almost impossible even *with* powers.                                                                                                                              |
| 9-10             | Incredible           | What is happening strains credulity so far you're having a hard time believing your eyes, dreamworld or not.                                                       |
| 11+              | Unimaginable         | You literally cannot imagine this happening.                                                                                                                       |

- \*There's no mechanical penalty for rolling negative Belief, just a suggestion to integrate it into your storytelling: your character might start spiraling, have a crisis of confidence, or approach similar checks with some trepidation in the future.

# Health and Damage

- HP = XRRPG HP divided by two (rounded down).
- Dealing Damage: Willpower contest, person who wins deals the other the difference in Belief.
- Reaching 0 HP means you only use your Base Dice Pool for every check.

# Round Order

1. Action Stage
	- All Acts except **Willpower**
	- You may take a penalty to do an Act quickly
		- Sacrifice any amount of d8, the Act gains Speed equal to that amount
		- Act with the highest Speed goes first
	- Can do a **Help** Act to give a teammate +1d8 on their next Act
	- For contesting Acts, the one with the higher Belief wins
2. **Willpower** Stage
	- Deal and take damage
3. Ritual Stage
	- Any Act done towards completing a ritual 
		- Rituals almost always require several Acts over more than one round
	- Can **Heal**, **Bind**, **Weaken**, and much more with [[Rituals]]
