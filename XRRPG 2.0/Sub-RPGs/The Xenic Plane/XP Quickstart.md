Your character details are the same as in the physical realm, so if you haven't made a character go to the [[Character Creation Template]] and write down or copy Basic Character Info and Stats, and then go to the [[Quick Start Guide]] for details. You'll only need Chroma, Stats, Species, one Class (feel free to just pick one that looks cool, you won't need all the details), and HP.

For more detailed Xenic Plane Mechanics click [[XP Mechanics|here]].

# Stats

- [[XP Mechanics#Base Dice Pool|Basic Dice Pool]]
	- Take the following Base Dice Pool depending on what your XRRPG (physical realm) Level is:
		- Level 1-3 --> 1d8 Base Dice Pool
		- Level 4-6 --> 2d8 Base Dice Pool
		- Level 7-9 --> 3d8 Base Dice Pool
- [[XP Mechanics#Basic Acts|Basic Acts]]
	- Gain an amount of Stat Points equal to your stats in the physical realm, and then allocate those points to [[XP Mechanics#Basic Acts|Basic Acts]] whose stat match the Stat Points you have.
		- If you have any negative stats go to the second paragraph of [[XP Mechanics#Basic Acts]] .
		- If you ever do anything in the xenic plane that doesn't fit into any of the Acts below just roll your Base Dice Pool.
	- Go to the bottom of [[XP Mechanics#Basic Acts|this]] section for a list of all the Basic Acts and what they do, and click [[XP Mechanics#Basic Act Examples|here]] for examples of each Basic Act.
- [[XP Mechanics#Special Acts|Special Acts]]
	- You gain a Special Act with level equal to its class level in the real world (max of 6).
	- Click [[XP Mechanics#Special Acts|here]] for a list of the Special Acts and what they do.
- [[XP Mechanics#Health|Health]]
	- You have Health equal to your HP stat in the physical realm divided by two (rounded down).

# Mechanics

- [[XP Mechanics#Belief|Belief]]
	- When you do anything you roll d8s (Base Dice Pool + any Basic Act or Special Act dice). Depending on what you roll it'll generate Belief:
		- 1s = -1
		- 2-4 = 0
		- 5-7 = +1
		- 8 = +2
	- Certain actions that you take will require more Belief to achieve; in the xenic plane you can do any action that you believe you can (provided something else isn't stopping you). Click [[XP Mechanics#Belief Results|here]] for what you can do given Belief generated.
	- Whenever you roll you generate [[XP Mechanics#Experience|Experience]], which equals all Belief rolled ignoring any negatives. You spend Experience to level up Acts, which allows you to roll more dice when doing that Act.
- [[XP Mechanics#Intense Situations|Intense Situations]]
	- When things get intense you fall into a sort of turn order—everybody chooses what they want to do before the round starts and then the Acts fall into this order:
		- Action Stage --> All Acts except **Willpower**.
			- Can take a penalty to do an Act quickly, sacrificing d8s for Speed (highest Speed goes first).
			- All Acts of the same Speed happen at the same time.
			- Can do a **Help** Act to give a teammate +1d8 on their next Act.
			- For contesting Acts, the one with the higher Belief wins
		- **Willpower** Stage --> [[XP Mechanics#Dealing and Taking Damage|Deal and take damage.]]
		- Ritual Stage --> Any Act done towards completing a [[Rituals|ritual]].
