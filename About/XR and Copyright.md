# My Guarantee

The worldbuilding, gameplay systems, and general XR content will *always* be completely free under a Creative Commons BY-SA license.

> [!NOTE] What this means (in a nutshell):
> 
> You can distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon the Xeon Rapture Universe in any way you like, including for commercial reasons, as long as these adaptations are shared under the same terms (a CC BY-SA license).

I want this universe to be a playground for anybody who wants to be involved, and to do that I need to keep it as open as possible!

This Guarantee includes everything on the xeonrapture.com and app.xeonrapture.com websites.
