Xeon Rapture is a massive multimedia project that I started in 2020 with the goal of both creating the fictional world that I've always wanted and creating a sandbox for me to put all the rest of my projects.
  
About 4.5 years later I've made an obsidian vault that contains over 1,500 notes and 350,000 words of lore, plans, and reference for the world, its people, its magic and technology, and the rules that bind it all together.
  
The world is a place where I can create characters that make music, tell stories, or share their adventures with others! Or where I can get together with friends in-person and let them explore the world through the [[XRRPG 1.0 Overview|XRRPG]].
  
The long-term goal is to invite other people to create characters in this world so they can participate in it as well, like some kind of long-form online role-playing game. But that's in the future; all I want right now is to be able to build my own projects in this universe.

As for me personally, I'm just very passionate about worldbuilding, music, and TTRPGs and want to contribute to a collaborative spirit. The world needs more beauty in it and I want be part of making it more beautiful, as well as create places for others to express their feelings and create beauty themselves.
