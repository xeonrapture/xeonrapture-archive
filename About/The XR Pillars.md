1. The Xeon Rapture Universe is a work of collaborative storytelling
2. The XRU belongs to everyone, and any worldbuilding done in it belongs to everyone
3. Everything made in it is canon (unless it blatantly breaks the rules, in which case it's an AU)
4. Be respectful and civil to all other storytellers; assume good faith
5. The XRU has no firm rules; boldness and creativity should be rewarded
