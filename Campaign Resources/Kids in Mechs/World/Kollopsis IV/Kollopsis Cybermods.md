# Intro

Welcome to cybermodding! To get any cybermods at all you need to get small neural cybermod that helps connect the mods to your mind. These neural cybermods are cheaper and can include a cyberport for no extra price.

# Styles of Mods

- Basic: fairly low price, but they look pretty cheap. Nuts and bolts.
- High-end: fine tech, takes inspiration from bio, self healing and more tough and efficient than bio, and has senses like bio
  - There's also high end that is just metal, but it blends in more (like cyberpunk mods), from a different company
- Exozinik: all held together with xeon binding, very expensive, custom-made for you
- Pbosh: heavy metals, the most durable, high level of craftsmanship
